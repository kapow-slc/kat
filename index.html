Trip Tips
🚗 Car Rental Handoff
Thrifty drop-off Apr 8 is at 9:00 AM — return the car first, then check bags for the 11:50 AM flight. Alamo drops off Apr 11 at 10:30 AM — return it on the way to the airport for your noon departure.
⚓ Pearl Harbor Tips (Thu, Apr 9)
Kids 4 and under are free. Reserve USS Arizona Memorial tickets at recreation.gov — they sell out weeks in advance. No bags allowed inside; use paid lockers. Plan 3–4 hrs total on site.
🚣 Mountain Tubing (Sun, Apr 5)
No flip flops — secured water shoes only. Reef-safe sunscreen required. Headlamps and gloves are provided. Lunch is included ~2 hrs into the tour. Min age 5, min height 43 inches.
